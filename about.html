About Me

My Story

My name is Kavita, I am a UX Designer originally from Mumbai, India but I am currently based in Austin, TX. I spend most of my free time hanging out with my friends, attending kick boxing sessions and spending time with my five-year-old daughter. My interest in discovering new things is what made my decision to transition into UX an easy one. My goal is to create and/or redesign platforms to ensure the best experience for the business and its clients!

Through all my experiences, I have enjoyed clean and simple designs. I want to bring that, the simplifying of complex ideas, to clients and users through my work and help bring their ideas to life.
